Administrator notice:

While we at Fur Affinity feel that fighting online piracy is important, the heavy-handed SOPA and PIPA bills proposed by the US Congress would have a chilling effect on our ability to conduct business.
Learn more about SOPA and PIPA.
If you live in the US, please contact your senators and representative and ask them to vote these bills down. The bill numbers are H.R. 3261 and S. 968.
If you live outside the US, please spread the word to your US-based friends and family. If you use sites with US-based domain names or US-based search engines, then these bills will affect you, too.
