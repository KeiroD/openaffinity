December Banners
   
  Posted by Fender
on 11/23/11 04:10 pm
For December, we're going to be rotating banners out every few days to celebrate the holidays! This will give quite a few people a chance to get noticed.

Requirements: Must be 525x125 pixels.

Must contain either of the mascots (Fender or Rednef). Please do not include admins in the banner, but a personal character is welcome so long as they're not the focus (e.g. in the background). MUST be PG-13, should be fun and expressive, and relevant to Winter (as opposed to just Christmas).

Just upload it with the keyword WinterBanner2011. While we can't use every banner submit, we'll pick those we feel are represent the spirit of the community.
